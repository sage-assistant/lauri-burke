Season One

Episode Guide

Six cinematic chapters documenting the most compelling coaching migration ever recorded on the African continent.

Episode 1 48 min
First Contact
The initial discovery of Homo Transcendicus Burkensis in its natural habitat.
The research team first encounters the Burke at a Cape Town coffee shop, where she has been documented converting skeptics into believers in a single conversation. "We were simply observing the local fauna," recalls lead researcher Dr. Hendriks, "when we noticed every human within a five meter radius suddenly sitting up straighter and making intense eye contact with their own reflections." The Burke had begun coaching. There was no stopping it.
Episode 2 52 min
The SACAP Years
Years spent studying human behavioral patterns at the prestigious SACAP research facility.
Before the Burke could coach the world, she first had to understand it. This episode chronicles her years at the South African College of Applied Psychology, where she immersed herself in applied psychology, counselling, and coaching. Fellow students report that study groups with the Burke always ended with participants accidentally achieving personal breakthroughs. "I came to review chapter seven," one classmate recalls. "I left having forgiven my father."
Episode 3 55 min
The Cape Town Incident
When an entire neighbourhood began exhibiting signs of spontaneous personal growth.
In what scientists now call "The Cape Town Incident," residents of a four block radius surrounding the Burke's coaching practice began displaying extraordinary behavioral changes. Productivity increased 340%. Journaling supplies sold out across three suburbs. A local barista reported that customers stopped ordering "the usual" and started ordering "what they actually wanted." Authorities were baffled. The Burke was simply doing her job.
Episode 4 50 min
The Level Up Call
Documenting the Burke's signature vocalization and its extraordinary effects on nearby humans.
Every species has its signature call. For the Burke, it is the phrase "Level Up," a vocalization so potent it has been known to cause immediate shifts in perspective, posture, and life trajectory. Sound engineers attempted to isolate the frequency. They could not. "It's not the words," concluded Dr. Patel. "It's the conviction behind them. Also, possibly magic. We cannot rule out magic."
Episode 5 58 min
There Is No Going Back
Following subjects who entered the Burke Effect radius and emerged permanently transformed.
This episode follows twelve individuals who entered what researchers now call "the Burke Effect radius" and found themselves unable to return to their previous level of existence. "I tried to go back to not caring about my potential," says Subject #23, visibly emotional. "I can't. She ruined mediocrity for me. I am devastatingly inspired and I cannot turn it off." The research team notes that not a single subject has filed a complaint. Most have sent thank you cards.
Episode 6 62 min
The Migration Continues
As the Burke's influence expands beyond Cape Town, the world braces for impact.
In the season finale, satellite imagery reveals that the Burke's coaching radius is expanding. Instagram account @level_up_with_lauri has become a digital migration route, drawing humans from across the globe into her field of influence. "We thought it was contained to Cape Town," whispers a visibly shaken researcher. "We were wrong. She has WiFi now. Nobody is safe from becoming their best self."
